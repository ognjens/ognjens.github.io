Our process

There’s method to our madness. We’re not looking for temporary fixes but rather long-term contributors who will feel comfortable as one of our own. Don't sweat it, we're not into interrogations we just want to get to know you.

1

Lets talk

We start with a phone call for a first chat so we can introduce TouchTunes and get to know more about you.

2

Come for a visit

Come visit our offices for a 2nd chat and get a feel for our company culture.

3

Meet the crew

The 3rd meet is all about you. You can request to meet specific players and potential teammates and see what they are working on.

4

Join us

You like us. We like you. Welcome aboard!
